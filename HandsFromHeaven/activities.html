This project aims to empower and support persons with disabilities by providing them with access to essential resources, services, and opportunities. Through innovative initiatives and inclusive approaches, we strive to create a more accessible and inclusive environment that enables persons with disabilities to lead fulfilling lives and actively participate in society. Our Hospital Support Project is dedicated to improving healthcare services and facilities for communities in need. We collaborate with hospitals and medical centers to enhance their capacity, infrastructure, and resources, ensuring they can deliver high-quality care to patients. From upgrading medical equipment to providing training for healthcare professionals, our efforts are geared towards improving access to healthcare services and promoting better health outcomes for all. Together, we strive to build healthier and stronger communities by ensuring access to quality healthcare for everyone.view more...



The Mobility Assistance Project aims to enhance the mobility and independence of people who face challenges with walking. Through the provision of assistive devices, such as wheelchairs, walkers, and mobility aids, we enable individuals to navigate their surroundings with greater ease and confidence. Additionally, our project focuses on advocating for accessible infrastructure and inclusive design principles to create environments that accommodate the needs of people with walking disabilities. By promoting mobility and accessibility, we aim to empower individuals to live active and fulfilling lives, irrespective of their mobility limitations.view more...
