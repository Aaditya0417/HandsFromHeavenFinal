Learn more about Hands From Heaven

Hands From Heaven is dedicated to providing shelter, food, and education to homeless individuals and families in need.

  • We offer temporary housing and support services to help individuals transition to permanent housing.
  • We recruit certified NGO's for our cause.
  • We aspire to change the lives of many individuals through the funds raised

Join us in our mission to create a better world for all. Together, we can make a difference!
