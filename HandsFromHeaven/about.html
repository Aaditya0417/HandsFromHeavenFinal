About Us

At Hands From Heaven, we believe in making a difference in the lives of people who are in need. Our organization is dedicated to making sure that the essential resources and support reach the correct places. We are committed to facilitating efficient fund allocation from companies to various NGOs. Our centralized platform fosters collaboration and transparency, ensuring that financial support reaches NGOs with projects aligned with specific causes.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet profound: to extend a helping hand to those who need it the most. Our mission is to streamline the fund allocation process, empowering stakeholders with a robust and transparent platform for effective collaboration and decision-making in the fund allocation process.

What We Do

  • Facilitate efficient fund allocation from companies to various NGOs
  • Foster collaboration and transparency in the fund allocation process
  • Ensure financial support reaches NGOs with projects aligned with specific causes
  • Empower stakeholders with a robust and transparent platform for effective collaboration and decision-making

Our Values

  • Integrity - We uphold the highest standards of integrity and transparency in everything we do.
  • Impact - We are driven by the desire to create a meaningful and lasting impact on the lives of children, families, and communities.
  • Efficiency - We strive for efficiency in fund allocation and management
  • Compassion - We approach our work with empathy and compassion, understanding the needs of non-profits.
  • Collaboration - We believe in the power of collaboration and work closely with partners and stakeholders to maximize our impact.
  • Innovation - We continuously seek innovative solutions to address the evolving needs of NGOs
  • Empowerment - We empower stakeholders to make informed decisions and take action
