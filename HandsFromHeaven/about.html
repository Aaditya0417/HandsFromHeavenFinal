Help everyone discover the joy of living and join us in our efforts to put Every Child in School.

Hands From Heaven is committed to making a difference in the lives of children in need. Our mission is to provide shelter, food, and education to those who need it the most.

Volunteer Benefits

As a volunteer, you will receive the following benefits:

  1. Training - discounted rates on unique training courses.
  2. Events - discounted tickets and invitations to members-only events.
  3. Community - share knowledge and best practices with peers.
  4. Networking - connect with the right people when you need to.
  5. Expertise - access to Bond groups, tools, and resources.
